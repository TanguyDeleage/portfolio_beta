Les 400 Coop

Les 400 Coop is a French cooperative located in Paris 11. Cooperators choose to work their 3h a month to be able to do their groceries their. The cooperative uses a website to inform and to help members to manage their obligations.

Challenge

Currently 400 Coop is loosing cooperators (less revenue, but also less people helping), it needs to attract new people. As a cooperator I choose to help them attract new people by working on the design of their websites.

The problem

The main website's goal should be communication. First to encourage potential future cooperators to register and then provide informations. Engagements must be clearly display and it must provides all the informations needed. In addition the future cooperator must understand the benefits it will receive.

Discovery

My first action was to understand how their current website is build

My first analysis is that the home page is full of informations of different importances (timetables, documentations, link to register, how it works, historical facts). Some links and informations are displayed several times and in various formats. In my opinion it seems essential to work on a simpler flow that will help someone to register and get the informations he needs.

I started this project really recently and I'm currently inside the discovery phase. I will soon update the informations.
