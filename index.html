About

Six years ago, I chose to pursue an academic path in management. During this period, I had many experiences : soft and hard skills trainings, travels and meetings. Thanks to these experiences I discovered the job field named "Product".

A first internship allowed me to connect my academic program to the product environment. During 6 month I was Product Owner for a French telecom leader. Then I chose to become more technical so I joined the full stack developer training of Le Wagon, as a member of bootcamp #659. Thanks to this training I discovered a passion for product design, which I continue to study as a autodidactically.

With a managerial background, an experience in product, development skills, and an attraction for design, I am now moving towards a role of product designer.
