About

A first internship allowed me to connect my academical program to the product environment throught a Product Owner role for a French telecom leader. Then I then choosed to become more technical so I joined the bootcamp #659 of Le Wagon, a fullstack developer training. Thanks to this training I discovered and initiated myself to design, which I continue to study as a self-taught.

With a managerial background, an experience in product, development skills, and an attraction for design, I am now moving towards a role of product designer.
