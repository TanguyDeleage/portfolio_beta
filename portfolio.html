About

Six years ago, I chose to pursue an academic career in management. During this period, I was enriched by experiences : trainings, trips and meetings. Thanks to these experiences I discovered a new job field name "Product".

A first internship allowed me to connect this new discovery to my academical program throught a Product Owner role for a French leader in telecoms.

I then choosed to become more technical so I joined the bootcamp #659 of Le Wagon, a fullstack developer training. Thanks to this training I discovered and initiated myself to design, which I continue to study as a self-taught.

With a managerial background, experience in product, development skills, and an attraction for design, I am now moving towards a role of product designer.
