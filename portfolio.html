About

My name is Tanguy. Six years ago, I chose to pursue an academic career in management. During this period, I was enriched by many trips, meetings and trainings. Thanks to these experiences I discovered the world of the product. A first internship allowed me to get a foot in this world but in order to explore this new environment, I decided to go beyond the framework set by a school program and joined the bootcamp #659 of Le Wagon (a fullstack developer training). There I discovered and initiated myself to design, which I continue to study as a self-taught. With a managerial background, experience in product, development skills, and an attraction for design, I am now moving towards a role of product designer.
